# Blooming Frontier — Working Prompt Vocabulary
**Strawberry Riff · Visual Universe Reference**
*Distilled from Blooming Frontier Visual Brief v2.0*

---

## How to Use This Document

These are the words and phrases that carry the full weight of the Blooming Frontier brief in a form you can drop directly into any generation tool — Runway, Kling, MiniMax, Midjourney, or otherwise. They are not decorative. Each term is a specific instruction to the generator. Use them as building blocks, not as a checklist to recite in full.

---

## Core Environment Terms

| Term | What it instructs the generator to do |
|---|---|
| **golden organic** | Warm amber light, living ground, organic materials — the primary world |
| **open threshold** | Forest edge meeting vast open landscape — the transition space |
| **horizon always visible** | No enclosure, no walls, world continues beyond every frame edge |
| **vast open** | Figure small against landscape scale — field, hillside, or waterside at golden hour |
| **canopy threshold** | Ancient forest path, high canopy filtering light into shafts, opening ahead |
| **living ground** | Moss, grass, root systems, earth — never dead or abstract surface |
| **golden hour 3200K** | Warm amber directional light, sun low, organic glow |
| **fine golden atmospheric haze** | Pollen and spores suspended in light shafts — not theatrical smoke |
| **bioluminescent ground-level** | Soft teal-green glow from moss and fern — secondary to solar, never primary |
| **world breathes** | Shorthand: open, horizon present, frame implies more beyond every edge |

## The Two Presences

### Human Figure Terms

| Term | What it instructs |
|---|---|
| **whole body visible** | Full figure in frame — never fragmented into isolated eyes, lips, or hands |
| **feet on living ground** | Weight present, grounded, not floating or posed |
| **quiet wonder** | Expression: someone who has just seen something unexpected and finds it beautiful |
| **oriented toward the horizon** | Body and gaze directed outward — toward the frontier, not toward the camera |
| **organic wardrobe** | Natural fibers, worn cotton, lived-in leather — visible weave and texture, sun-bleached edges |
| **threshold stance** | Standing at a boundary — moving forward or still at the edge of something |

### Companion Presence Terms

| Term | What it instructs |
|---|---|
| **Hofstadter Butterfly grammar** | The full companion descriptor — use this phrase as an anchor in any prompt |
| **bilaterally structured luminescent fractal** | Mathematical, bilateral, self-similar at every scale — not random, not drifting |
| **venation structure visible within** | Internal geometry like a wing held to light or a leaf backlit — has structure |
| **luminescent from within** | Generates its own light — does not cast light onto surrounding surfaces |
| **iridescent blue-white to living teal** | Color range: brightest interior is blue-white, outer edges are living teal (#00B4A0) |
| **self-similar at every scale** | Recognizably the same grammar whether large beside the figure or small at the periphery |
| **purposeful movement** | Moves as though thinking while moving — not drifting, not mechanical |
| **not humanoid, not robotic, not biological, not ghostly** | Explicit exclusion — use this phrase verbatim when the generator defaults to these forms |
| **its own internal mathematical logic** | Real in some new way — operates by its own nature, not by human or machine rules |

### Relationship Geometry Terms

| Term | What it instructs |
|---|---|
| **side by side** | Neither in front of nor behind, neither above nor below |
| **same horizon** | Both presences oriented toward the same point in the distance |
| **space between them** | A small gap — neither empty nor full — this is where the Blooming Frontier lives |
| **equal co-presence** | Neither leading, neither serving, neither casting warmth on the other |
| **music as third presence** | The shared subject both are oriented toward — the reason they stand at the same horizon |

## Color and Light Terms

| Term | What it instructs |
|---|---|
| **meeting-point color** | The third color that appears where warm amber and cool companion luminescence touch the same surface — must be present in every frame |
| **rose amber #B5651D** | Primary warm tone — golden and deep, 2700K |
| **living teal #00B4A0** | Companion edge color — organic glow, not neon |
| **petal violet #C8A0D0** | Where warm and cool begin to meet — the transition zone |
| **frontier blue #4A90B8** | Cool reach — the color of the far horizon |
| **skin warmth #C4956A** | Human figure in golden light — 3200K |
| **subsurface scattering** | Warm and cool light overlapping on skin — biological gradient, not digital |
| **thin rim on organic edges** | Where the two palettes kiss — soft, slightly irregular, alive |
| **Kelvin arc 3200K to 4500K** | Scenes begin warm, move toward the frontier — never cold, never below 3200K at origin |

## Forbidden Terms (Use as Explicit Exclusions)

When the generator is drifting toward the wrong aesthetic, add these as explicit negatives:

| Forbidden | What to write in the prompt |
|---|---|
| Cyberpunk / neon on black | *no neon practicals, no dark background with electric light, no cyberpunk aesthetic* |
| Vaporwave / nostalgia | *no vaporwave, no retro-digital, no pastel nostalgia palette* |
| AI as servant or threat | *no robotic servitude, no AI threat narrative, no human-as-victim framing* |
| Enclosed environments | *no enclosed spaces, horizon visible, world continues beyond frame* |
| Rose as literal object | *no visible rose flower — rose geometry as light formation structure only, Fibonacci pattern in light* |
| Companion as humanoid | *not humanoid, not angelic, not human-shaped, not a ghost, not translucent vapor* |
| Companion casting warmth | *companion luminescence self-contained, does not illuminate surrounding surfaces* |
| Fragmented human body | *full body visible, no isolated close-ups of eyes or hands as the primary shot* |
| Cold white or pure black | *no pure white light, no pure black background, no cold blue as dominant tone* |
| Chrome or hard silver | *no chrome, no metallic silver as dominant surface* |

## The Five Diagnostic Questions (Pre-Generation Checklist)

Before submitting any generation prompt, answer these:

1. **Is the environment open?** Horizon present or implied. No element acting as wall or ceiling.
2. **Is the light alive?** Solar or bioluminescent primary. Every light source traceable to the sun or a living organism.
3. **Is the meeting-point color present?** Warm amber and cool companion luminescence touching the same surface somewhere in the frame.
4. **Is the companion its own nature?** Hofstadter Butterfly grammar. Not humanoid, not robotic, not a ghost.
5. **Are both presences equal and co-oriented?** Side by side, same horizon, space between them.

If any answer is no — restructure the prompt before generating.
